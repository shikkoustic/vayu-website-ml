How does this forecast work?

We use satellite imagery to measure current pollution levels, then combine it with live weather forecasts (wind, rain, temperature) to predict how air quality will change.

What drives these trends:

  • 🌬️ Wind — Strong winds disperse pollutants, improving AQI
  • 🌧️ Rain — Washes particles from the air
  • 🌡️ Temperature — Heat creates mixing, cold traps pollution near ground
  • 🕐 Time of day — Evening calm often worsens air quality

⚠️ These are AI estimates and may differ from actual readings, especially for hours further ahead. Use as a general guide, not medical advice.
