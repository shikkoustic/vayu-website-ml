About Project Vayu

Vayu is a student-led initiative dedicated to forecasting air quality and providing actionable insights in the simplest, most accurate way possible.

By fusing satellite-based data with advanced machine learning models (like XGBoost, LightGBM, and CatBoost), we process live meteorological patterns to accurately predict the Air Quality Index for specific local regions.

Current Focus

Currently based in Delhi, analyzing multiple regional zones including North, South, East, West, and Central Delhi.

Future Vision

We are continuously refining our models and look forward to advancing this venture to other major cities across the country.

*Our insights are subject to natural variation, and forecasted data may have slight error margins.
